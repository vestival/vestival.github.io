Then Web3 happened, and I jumped in. Since 2022 I've been leading product marketing at some of the most recognised protocols in the space: Chainlink, Polkadot, Polygon, and now Algorand Foundation.

Whether it's positioning a cloud management product for enterprise IT or helping developers understand why they should build on a new blockchain, I love making complex things click.
